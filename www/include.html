Analysis of the sample Insurance Data.

Thie project analyzes the insurance data to look for a patterns in the data and gain the insight of the Dataset that contains the medical charges for the individual wit their personal details.

Let's understand the dataset and closer look into the attributes of the dataset.
Attribute Description
Age age of the individual
sex Gender of the individual (0=female, 1=male).
bmi Body mass index BMI
children Number of children covered under health insurance / number of dependants.
smoker Indicates whether the person smokes or not.
charges Medical costs borne by the health insurance provider for the individual.
